When it comes to Japanese comfort food, few dishes match the irresistible charm of gyukatsu — a tender, juicy beef cutlet coated in golden breadcrumbs and fried to perfection. At Gyukatsu Motomura, we’ve turned this classic into a dining experience you’ll never forget, combining authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and an unwavering passion for quality.

Our Story

Gyukatsu Motomura began with one mission: to create the best beef cutlet in Japan. From the start, our focus has been on using only premium cuts of beef, preparing them with care, and preserving the traditional cooking techniques that make gyukatsu so special. Over the years, this dedication has earned us a loyal following among locals and travelers alike.

For those special moments, we offer private dining spaces perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate gatherings. Guests can enjoy a personalized menu, attentive service, and a relaxed setting tailored to the occasion.

Golden crust, a gentle bite, Juicy center, pure delight. Sizzling warmth, aroma sweet, Every slice—a perfect treat. Rice and miso by its side, Flavors blend, a joyful ride. Gyukatsu’s charm, forever true, A taste of Japan, made for you.– Ōgon no Kokoro –

Our restaurants are designed for warmth and comfort. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet solo lunch, a lively dinner with friends, or hosting a private event, our spaces are welcoming and cozy. We believe that great food should be shared and remembered — every visit is a chance to create lasting memories.

Whether you’re a local foodie or a traveler exploring Japan’s culinary treasures, Gyukatsu Motomura offers a taste you won’t soon forget. From the first bite to the last, every detail is crafted to bring joy and satisfaction. Make your reservation today and discover why so many call us the ultimate destination for gyukatsu lovers in Japan.

Tips for the Best Experience

Reserve Early: We’re often fully booked, especially during peak hours. Try the Variations: Cheese-filled, garlic, and spicy gyukatsu are crowd favorites. Pair with Sides: Seasonal vegetables and extra dipping sauces add flavor layers. Leave Room for Dessert: Ask about our limited-time seasonal sweets.
