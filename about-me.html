About Us

Gyukatsu Motomura

Restaurant

PHONE: +81927536229

Every plate at Gyukatsu Motomura tells a story of dedication and craftsmanship. From the careful preparation of each cut of beef to the perfectly crispy coating, our chefs work with passion to ensure every meal is as memorable as it is delicious.

At Gyukatsu Motomura, we bring the authentic taste of Japanese beef cutlet to life. Using carefully selected ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, we create dishes that honor our culinary heritage while delivering a rich, flavorful experience for every guest who dines with us.

We believe great dining is about more than just food. At Gyukatsu Motomura, we offer heartfelt hospitality in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. Our team strives to make every guest feel comfortable, valued, and eager to return for another unforgettable meal.
